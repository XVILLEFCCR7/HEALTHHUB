Your ultimate health tracking application. Keep track of your BMI, monitor your sleep, track your water intake, check your pulse rate, and use our converter for all your health-related calculations.

BMI

Calculate your Body Mass Index based on height and weight. A healthy BMI is typically between 18.5 and 24.9.

Sleep

Track your sleep patterns. Adequate sleep is essential for overall health, with adults needing around 7-9 hours per night.

Water

Monitor your water intake. It's recommended to drink at least 8 glasses of water per day to stay hydrated.

Pulse

Check your pulse rate. A normal resting heart rate for adults ranges from 60 to 100 beats per minute.
